Molika Hotel


Designed for both business and leisure travel, Molika Hotel is ideally situated in City Center; one of the city's most popular locales. Only 5.0 Km away, this 3-star hotel can be easily accessed from the airport. For those of you who want to venture out, Kampung Chhnang Caves, Central Market, KPC Souvenir Shop are just some of the attractions available to visitors. Molika Hotel offers impeccable service and all the essential amenities to invigorate travelers. 24-hour room service, free Wi-Fi in all rooms, 24-hour front desk, facilities for disabled guests, express check-in/check-out are just a few of the facilities that set Molika Hotel apart from other hotels in the city. Hotel accommodations have been carefully appointed to the highest degree of comfort and convenience. In some of the rooms, guests can find television LCD/plasma screen, clothes rack, linens, mirror, towels. The complete list of recreational facilities is available at the hotel, including garden. Molika Hotel is your one-stop destination for quality hotel accommodations in Kampung Chhnang.
